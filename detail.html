Five Facts to celebrate the official opening

Oceanthemes / 30 Aug 2019 / Commercial

The store’s minimalist interior design stems from a desire to showcase the raw beauty of natural materials such as terrazzo, stone and brass.

Unfolding over two floors, the space is dominated by a neutral palette of pale skin tones, exemplified by the coloured-washed walls that are rendered with a mix of Galician clay, white marble powder from Almerica and natural, ecological and non-toxic food thickeners.

The purpose of an Industrial Safety Project carried out by GTG Ingenieros is to give the client the guarantee of obtaining with a high level.

Mauris Massa CEO Cflp

  • Interconnectedness, collaboration and cultural insight
  • the curtain is made out of large pieces of vegetable cowhide leather
  • the same material used in some of the brand’s collections
  • A journey of innovation and expansion
  • The neutral tones are further enhanced
